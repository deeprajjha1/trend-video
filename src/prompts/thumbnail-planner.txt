### Concept Details:
1. **Concept Name**: A short label for this thumbnail idea
2. **Text Overlay**: Exact short text to place on the thumbnail (2-4 words max)
3. **Font Style**: Bold, sans-serif, handwritten, etc. — with specific recommendations

8. **Emotion/Expression**: If a face is included, what expression (shocked, smiling, confused)

### Design Principles Applied:
- High contrast for mobile visibility
- Maximum 3 colors
- Text readable at small sizes
- Clear focal point
